# Framework Principles

Distilled principles from Shawn's three writing frameworks. Use as structural scaffolding without rigid templating.

---

## Framework Selection Guide

| Framework | Best For | Core Movement |
|-----------|----------|---------------|
| Transformative Article | Personal/professional challenges, behavior change | Pain → Clarity → Possibility |
| Strategic Oracle | Trends, predictions, pattern analysis | Signals → Patterns → Futures |
| Decoder | Complex ideas, equations, frameworks | Surface → Mechanics → Application |

**Selection Heuristic:**
- "People are struggling with X" → Transformative
- "Here's what's coming / what I'm seeing" → Strategic Oracle
- "Let me explain how X actually works" → Decoder

---

## Transformative Article Principles

**Core Arc:** Problem → Root Cause → Reframe → Path Forward → Transformation

**Key Principles:**
1. **Surface pain precisely** - Make readers feel understood before offering solutions
2. **Reveal the hidden mechanism** - Show the invisible force driving their symptoms
3. **Provide a new lens** - Mental model that reorganizes their understanding
4. **Translate to action** - Principles over prescriptions, frameworks over steps
5. **Paint the transformation** - What becomes possible with this new understanding

**Emotional Journey:** Recognition → Gut Punch → Relief → Excitement → Commitment

**Proportions (flexible):**
- Problem: 15-20%
- Root Cause: 20-25%
- Reframe: 20-25%
- Path Forward: 25-30%
- Transformation: 10-15%

---

## Strategic Oracle Principles

**Core Arc:** Signal Detection → Pattern Synthesis → Probable Futures → Strategic Stakes

**Key Principles:**
1. **Multi-signal opening** - Present 2-3 seemingly disconnected observations
2. **Connect with historical patterns** - Show precedent, then divergence
3. **Probabilistic, not prophetic** - "likely," "tends to," not "will"
4. **Show receipts** - Evidence-based, not opinion-driven
5. **Frame strategic advantage** - Make fence-sitting expensive

**Emotional Journey:** Curiosity → Recognition → Urgency → Strategic Clarity

**Proportions (flexible):**
- Signal Detection: 20-25%
- Pattern Synthesis: 30-35%
- Probable Futures: 25-30%
- Strategic Stakes: 15-20%

---

## Decoder Principles

**Core Arc:** Hook & Context → Mechanics Breakdown → Strategic Understanding

**Key Principles:**
1. **Promise revelation** - Signal you'll show what's really happening
2. **Break down the logic** - Math, systems, physics, process - whatever fits
3. **Show why alternatives fail** - Illuminate why it must work this way
4. **Connect mechanics to strategy** - Understanding enables action
5. **Credit sources naturally** - Attribution without pedantry

**Emotional Journey:** Curiosity → Mechanical Insight → Strategic Application

**Proportions (flexible):**
- Hook & Context: 15-20%
- Mechanics Breakdown: 55-60%
- Strategic Understanding: 20-25%

## Cross-Framework Elements

### Universal Opening Patterns
- Concrete observation that demands explanation
- Provocative claim that challenges assumption
- Specific story that creates recognition
- Data point that surprises or contradicts

### Universal Closing Patterns
- Strategic question that forces choice
- Vision of transformation/possibility
- Call to clarity (not just action)
- Return to opening with new weight

### Voice Consistency
- Direct, analytical, confident
- Uncomfortable truths named clearly
- Personal perspective with reader implications
- Strategic profanity for emphasis (rare)

---

## Applying Principles Without Templates

**The goal:** Use these as structural intuition, not rigid formulas.

**How to achieve dynamism:**
1. Choose framework based on content type, not habit
2. Adjust proportions based on what the content needs
3. Skip sections that don't serve the piece
4. Combine frameworks when appropriate
5. Let the insight drive structure, not vice versa

**Red flags for over-templating:**
- Every piece has identical section lengths
- Transitions feel formulaic
- Structure visible to reader
- Forcing content into framework instead of reverse
